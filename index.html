Ardaku

General-purpose WebAssembly application engine. With Ardaku, you can run WebAssembly apps on any OS without a browser with full multi-threading support. You can forget building your app for Linux, MacOS, Windows, Android and iOS, and just build your app for Ardaku - and it will be able to run anywhere.
